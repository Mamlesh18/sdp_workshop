General Instructions



  • Maximum number of participants: 50
  • Eligibility for participants:
    • Students from interdisciplinary fields of science, engineering, and technology.
    • Students from polytechnic colleges.
    • Working professionals
    • Teaching faculty.
    • Scholars registered in Ph.D. programs.
  • Registration charges per participant: Rs.1500/- + 18 % GST
  • Participants must bring their laptops for joining the hands-on sessions.
  • A test will be conducted (online/offline) on the last day of the workshop and those who score more than 60% will receive Digital Certificate Issued by IIT Hyderababad.
  • Lunch and Refreshments will be provided to the participants.
  • No accommodation will be provided to the participants.
